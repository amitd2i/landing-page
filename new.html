artist and open source collaborators

Infinidream is a platform for generative artists, programmers, prompters, and dreamers of all stripes.

It can be as simple as uploading videos the same way you would to YouTube or Instagram, but with a better experience for your audience.
Go deeper and collaborate with AI by using our Python API to create infinite artworks.
Use Infinidream to tap into crowdsourcing or social interaction for participatory artworks.
Infinidream is open source and we invite collaboration of all kinds. Build on our platform, share your own art, or just report a bug.
Human artists and AI have complementary strengths and work best together as part of a collective intelligence.

roadmap

There are so many ways to expand and improve Infinidream. Currrently we are working on and thinking about:

Running on more platforms such as Windows, Android, iOS, tvOS, and directly on TVs and set-top boxes.
Managing multiple screens. Turn your home or space into an interactive gallery.
Social control: allowing the people around a screen to control it, not just the owner. Put a QR code next to a screen to make a video jukebox, or invent your own game.
Hosting our own cloud generation and make it driven by AI and crowd signals. Imagine putting an "edit" button on each dream and allowing you to change the prompts and make your own version.
Using the microphone to make playback audioreactive, detect the ambient activity level, and even ID any song. Add realtime effects, more mixing, and become an AI VJ.
